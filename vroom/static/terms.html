VROOM Car Rental Service Terms and Conditions

 

1. Insurance Coverage:

 

1.1. All rentals include basic insurance coverage, which typically covers damages to the rental vehicle in case of accidents, theft, or vandalism, subject to a deductible.

 

1.2. Additional insurance options, such as collision damage waiver (CDW) or personal accident insurance (PAI), may be available for purchase at an extra cost.

 

1.3. Renter is responsible for familiarizing themselves with the terms and coverage of the insurance policy provided and any additional insurance options.

 

2. Renter Responsibility:

 

2.1. The renter is responsible for the safe and lawful operation of the rental vehicle throughout the duration of the rental period.

 

2.2. Renter must return the vehicle in the same condition it was rented, with the exception of normal wear and tear.

 

2.3. Renter is liable for any damages, fines, or penalties incurred during the rental period, including but not limited to traffic violations, parking tickets, and tolls.

 

3. Price Negotiations:

 

3.1. Prices for rental vehicles are non-negotiable and are determined based on factors such as vehicle type, rental duration, and location.

 

3.2. VROOM reserves the right to adjust rental prices at any time without prior notice.

 

4. Available Locations:

 

4.1. VROOM operates rental locations in select cities and regions, as listed on our website or through our customer service representatives.

 

4.2. Availability of specific vehicle models may vary by location and is subject to change without prior notice.

 

5. Time Duration of Rental:

 

5.1. The rental period begins at the scheduled pickup time and ends at the scheduled return time agreed upon during the reservation process.

 

5.2. Extensions to the rental period may be possible, subject to vehicle availability and approval by VROOM.

 

5.3. Late returns may incur additional fees, charged on a pro-rated basis according to the hourly or daily rental rate.

 

6. Cancellation Policy:

 

6.1. Renters may cancel a reservation without penalty up to 3 hours after the reservation is made.

 

6.2. If cancellation occurs more than 3 hours after the reservation is made but at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled pickup time, a cancellation fee of 20% of the total rental cost will be charged.

 

6.3. In the event of a no-show (failure to pick up the rental vehicle at the scheduled time without prior notification), the full rental cost will be charged as a cancellation fee.

 

6.4. Cancellation requests must be submitted through the VROOM website or by contacting our customer service team directly.

 

6.5. VROOM reserves the right to waive cancellation fees under extenuating circumstances, such as documented medical emergencies or severe weather conditions.
